Chapter 3

Discussion for Food Festival / Surviving Skills

Food Festival Discussion

This session focused on our upcoming food festival. During the lecture, we discussed the skills needed for successful event management, including teamwork, communication, problem-solving, and adaptability in dynamic environments. At the end of the session, we named our stall "Cafe by Chandrapala", a unique name that we felt would stand out.

Surviving Skills Activity

After the discussion about the food festival, we did a small activity called "Surviving Skills". We were given the following scenario:

Your corporate team was selected for a winter leadership retreat in the Himalayas. While at Basecamp, an unexpected blizzard hits, cutting off all communication and access to help. You must decide which 8 of the 16 available items to take with you to survive and maintain team morale, safety, and functionality for at least 3 days until help arrives.

This activity was designed to focus on skills such as:

  • Leadership under pressure
  • Strategic decision-making
  • Role delegation
  • Consensus building
  • Risk management and teamwork

Instructions for the Activity

  1. Form groups of 4-6 students.
  2. Read the Blizzard at Basecamp scenario.
  3. Review the survival item list.
  4. Select only 8 items as a team.
  5. Justify each selected item: why it’s necessary, what skill it supports, and who suggested it.
  6. Assign roles: Team Leader, Recorder, Timekeeper, and Presenter.
  7. Present decisions to the class if time permits.
