Chapter 9 – Emotional Intelligence

Understanding emotions and their role in personal and social success

What is Emotion?

Emotion is a psychological state which involves:

  • Feeling component
  • Physiological response
  • Behavioural response

Types of Emotions

Humans express two types of emotions:

FearAnxiety

How People Show Emotions

  • Voice
  • Breathing
  • Energy level
  • Muscle tone and movement
  • Skin, sweating
  • Facial expression

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability to understand and manage your own emotions and those of others. It allows you to navigate social situations, read other people, and develop lasting relationships.

EQ vs IQ

IQ (Intelligence Quotient): Measures cognitive skills such as understanding, learning, reasoning, and problem-solving. Evaluated through memory exercises, analogies, and puzzles.

EQ (Emotional Quotient): Measures your ability to comprehend, control, and utilize emotions to achieve goals and build positive relationships.

Components of Emotional Intelligence

  • Social Skills: Communicating effectively in different situations
  • Empathy: Understanding others
  • Self-regulation: Mindfulness and acceptance
  • Self-awareness: Understanding yourself better than others
