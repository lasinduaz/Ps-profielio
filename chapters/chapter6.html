Chapter 6 – Food Festival

Experience of organizing and managing a campus food festival

Overview

A Food Festival was conducted by second-year, second-semester students on July 29, 2025, at the university premises during lunchtime. This was part of the Professional Skills module. The aim was to enhance leadership, marketing, and teamwork skills through a group assignment.

Our Stall – Chandarapala

Our team of 10 members ran a stall named "Chandarapala". We sold a variety of items including Spaghetti, Watalapan, Iced Coffee, Mojito, Waffles, Cake, and Fruit Kebab. Each member had specific responsibilities to ensure efficiency.

Preparation Timeline

  • Two weeks before: Assigned roles and finalized the menu.
  • One week before: Purchased all ingredients.
  • Day before: Cooked Waffles and Cake.
  • Morning of event: Prepared Spaghetti, Iced Coffee, Fruit Kebabs, and Watalapan.
  • 2 hours before: Three members decorated the stall.

Team Roles

Members were assigned tasks such as cashiering, transporting items, and decorating the stall. Our stall’s location at the building entrance helped attract attention and boosted sales.
